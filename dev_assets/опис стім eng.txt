[h1]Why "руп", "осада", "Швайцарія", etc?[/h1]
We use outdated words to convey the atmosphere of the world at that time. We also use Skrypnykivka (wish of the authors).
 

[h1]How to play with Ukrainian?[/h1]
[olist]
    [*]Download the translation
    [*]Choose [b]English[/b] in the launcher
    [*]Launch and enjoy the Ukrainian
[/olist]
