[h1]Ukrainian translation for Victoria 3.[/h1]
[b]To start the translation, select English in the launcher![/b]
[b]Work on the translation is still in progress, so you can often find the English text![/b]


[h1]Why "руп", "осада", "Швайцарія", etc?[/h1]
We use outdated words to convey the atmosphere of the world at that time. We also use [url=https://uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/%D0%A5%D0%B0%D1%80%D0%BA%D1%96%D0%B2%D1%81%D1%8C%D0%BA%D0%B8%D0%B9_%D0%BF%D1%80%D0%B0%D0%B2%D0%BE%D0%BF%D0%B8%D1%81]Skrypnykivka[/url] (wish of the authors).
 

[h1]How to play with Ukrainian?[/h1]

    [*]Download the translation

    [*]Launch and enjoy the Ukrainian

[h1]Translation progress.[/h1]
Currently translated:
[list]
[*]Most part of the interface
[*]The majority part of events, decisions, and entries in the original game's journal
[*]Part of the events, decisions, and entries in the Voice of the People expansion's journal
[*]All names of countries (and descriptions), cultures, religions, types of government
[*]All names of laws and descriptions of laws, production methods, character traits
[*]All song titles of the original game and the expansion "Melodies for the Masses"
[*]Part of tutorials
[*]Part of concepts
[*]Small part of character names
[/list]
